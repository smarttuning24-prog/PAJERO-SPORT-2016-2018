GENERAL INFORMATION


Three systems are used as below:

  • An Ultrasonic misacceleration Mitigation System is available as an option. This system detects an obstacle around the vehicle by using eight sensors (four on the front bumper, four on the rear bumper). When the system determines that the driver depresses the accelerator pedal abnormally or a collision is imminent, it will suppress the engine output.
  • A corner sensor function is also available as an option. Depending on an obstacle detection, the vehicle drive information and the distance to the obstacle, this function alerts the driver visually and aurally by using the sonar switch indicator and the multi information display.
  • A blind spot warning function is used. The clearance warning control unit recognises an obstacle around the vehicle by using four side sensors (two on the front bumper, two on the rear bumper). In addition, the clearance warning control unit obtains vehicle driving status from the ECUs. When the system detects a vehicle within the blind spots, the buzzers incorporated in the door mirrors and the clearance warning control unit sound to warn the driver.
