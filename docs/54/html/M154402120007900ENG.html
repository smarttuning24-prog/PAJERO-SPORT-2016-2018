Inspection Procedure 7: The USB adapter data cannot be replayed.


caution
  • Before replacing the multivision display, ensure that the power supply circuit, the earth circuit and the communication circuit are normal.
  • Check whether the multivision display supports your audio device or an audio file format.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the sound replay is impossible with the USB/iPod® mode, the USB/HDMI cable or multivision display may be defective.
note
  • Before troubleshooting, check that the music file (the customer tried to replay) can be replayed.
  • When any error is found in a connected iPod, first check whether it is an official product.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of multivision display
  • Malfunction of USB/HDMI cable
