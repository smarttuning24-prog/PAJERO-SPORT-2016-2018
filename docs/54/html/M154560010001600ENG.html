GENERAL INFORMATION


This system allows the driver to call a phone to a public safety answering point manually or automatically, thus mitigating a personal injury or a vehicle damage.

  • When the e-Call switch is pressed, the system will call the public safety answering point, thus allowing the driver to make an emergency call.
  • When the system detects a vehicle collision, it will give a call to the public safety answering point to inform of a current vehicle position and information.
