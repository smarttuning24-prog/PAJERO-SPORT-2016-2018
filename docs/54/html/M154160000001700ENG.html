GENERAL INFORMATION


220 V AC power outlet which can be used as power supply for home electric appliances has been adopted.



  • With the AC inverter, the DC power supply of the vehicle battery can be inverted into the AC power supply and provided from the AC inverter socket.
  • The electric appliance whose power consumption is 150 W or less when 220 V AC is supplied can be used.
    note The home appliance whose power consumption is 150 W or less but requires a large amount of power at startup (such as TV, refrigerator, vacuum cleaner) may not be used.
  • To prevent damages caused by overcurrent, overvoltage, low voltage, and overheat* when the power is supplied, the protection function to stop the power supply has been provided.
    note *: When the inside of the vehicle is held at high temperature for a long time (for example under the scorching sun), if the AC power supply is used with the vehicle parked, the operation may automatically stop to prevent the inverter unit from being overheated.
  • When the power supply stops, turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position once to inhibit the power supply to the inverter. After that, turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position again for return.
