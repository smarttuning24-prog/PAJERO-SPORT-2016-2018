REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

<<A>> COLUMN SWITCH ASSEMBLY <VEHICLES WITHOUT STEERING WHEEL SENSOR>/COLUMN SWITCH ASSEMBLY (INTEGRATED WITH STEERING WHEEL SENSOR) <VEHICLES WITH STEERING WHEEL SENSOR>/CLOCK SPRING REMOVAL

1.
caution The removed clock spring should be stored in a clean, dry place.
Lift the connector lock of clock spring connector to the direction of the arrow, then unlock and disconnect the connector.

2.Using a pliers or similar tool, pinch and lift the band clump pin shown in the figure.

3.With the flat-tipped screwdriver inserted into the column shaft groove shown in the figure, lift the claw shown in Section A - A, and then remove the column switch assembly by pulling out the column switch assembly.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT

>>A<< CLOCK SPRING INSTALLATION
Refer to GROUP 52B - Driver’s Air Bag Module and Clock Spring .

>>B<< COLUMN SWITCH ASSEMBLY <VEHICLES WITHOUT STEERING WHEEL SENSOR>/COLUMN SWITCH ASSEMBLY (INTEGRATED WITH STEERING WHEEL SENSOR) <VEHICLES WITH STEERING WHEEL SENSOR>/CLOCK SPRING INSTALLATION

1.Using a pliers or similar tool, pinch and lift the band clump pin shown in the figure.
2.Align the part "A" position in the figure with the steering lock side rib, and then insert the column switch assembly into the column shaft.
3.After mounting the column switch assembly by securely fitting the column switch assembly claw with the column shaft groove, release the band clump pin.
