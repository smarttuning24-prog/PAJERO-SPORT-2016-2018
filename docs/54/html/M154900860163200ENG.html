Inspection procedure 7: One of the door switch signals is not received.


caution Whenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input signal circuit is normal.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If there is an error to the rear door switch and tailgate latch switch input signal, the rear door switch signal is no longer output to the communication line.
note
  • When the right, left rear door or tailgate is opened, the signal is also displayed as "Rear door switch". Therefore, when "Rear door switch" signal is displayed as abnormal, you should determine which signal line is defective by opening the rear doors and tailgate one at a time.
  • On vehicles with the gateway-ECU, the rear door switch and the tailgate latch switch are connected to the gateway-ECU.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of rear door switch (LH)
  • Malfunction of rear door switch (RH)
  • Malfunction of tailgate latch switch
  • Malfunction of the gateway-ECU
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

STEP 6. Check the wiring harness between rear doors switch connector or tailgate latch connector and gateway-ECU connector.



  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DI14 line between rear door switch (LH) connector and gateway-ECU connector.
  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DI13 line between rear door switch (RH) connector and gateway-ECU connector.
  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DI15 line between tailgate latch connector and gateway-ECU connector.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the gateway-ECU (Refer to GROUP 54C - Gateway-ECU ).

Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.

STEP 7. Check the wiring harness between rear doors switch connector or tailgate latch connector and ETACS-ECU connector.



  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DOOR line between rear door switch (LH) connector and ETACS-ECU connector.
  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DOOR line between rear door switch (RH) connector and ETACS-ECU connector.
  • Check of short to earth, and open circuit in DOOR line between tailgate latch connector and ETACS-ECU connector.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.

Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
