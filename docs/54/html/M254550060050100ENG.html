FRONT SEAT BELT REMINDER FUNCTION
When the driver’s seat belt is released
  1. When the ignition switch is turned ON, the driver’s seat belt warning lamp illuminates and the buzzer sounds for 6 seconds.
  2. If the vehicle speed exceeds 8 km/h, the driver’s seat belt warning lamp illuminates and blinks for 90 seconds. The buzzer sounds simultaneously with the blinks of the warning lamp.
  3. On Step 2 completion, if the seat belt is not fastened when the vehicle speed changes from 3 km/h to 8 km/h, the warning lamp illuminates and blinks again and the buzzer sounds again.
When the front passenger’s seat belt is released
  • Control is the same as when the driver’s seat belt is not fastened.

FCM COLLISION WARNING BUZZER/FCM APPROACHING WARNING BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH FCM>
Sounds when the FCM activates.

A/T SHIFT CANCEL BUZZER <A/T>
The buzzer sounds when the shift change on the transmission side is not made although the downshift operation is performed with the selector lever or paddle shift.

HIGH OIL TEMP WARNING BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH PADDLE SHIFT>
This buzzer sounds when the automatic transmission fluid is hot.

ELECTRIC STEERING WHEEL LOCK WARNING BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH KOS>
A buzzer will sound when the steering wheel lock is not activated/deactivated normally.

OSS-RELATED MALFUNCTION WARNING BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH KOS>
A buzzer will sound when an error occurs in the KOS&OSS-ECU power supply system.

ENGINE SWITCH REMINDER BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH KOS>
With the engine switch in the ON position, when the driver’s door is opened, the buzzer sounds.

KEYLESS OPERATION KEY REMINDER BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH KOS>
The buzzer will sound when the driver’s door is opened while the keyless operation key is inserted into the key slot with the engine switch OFF.

LAMP REMINDER BUZZER
With the ignition switch in the LOCK (OFF) position, when the driver’s door is opened with the tail lamp switch remaining ON, the buzzer sounds.

DOOR-AJAR WARNING BUZZER
With the ignition switch in the ON position, when any of the doors (including the tailgate) is opened and the vehicle speed information sent via the CAN communication is 8 km/h or more, the buzzer sounds.

FREEZE WARNING BUZZER
When the ignition switch is in the ON position, if the ambient temperature is at 3°C or below, the buzzer sounds.

PARKING BRAKE REMINDER BUZZER
With the ignition switch in the ON position, if the parking brake has not been released (parking brake switch: ON), and when the vehicle speed information transmitted via CAN communication is 8 km/h or more, the buzzer sounds.

MULTI INFORMATION DISPLAY SWITCH OPERATION BUZZER <MONO-COLOUR LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY TYPE>
When the multi information display switch is operated, the buzzer sounds.

RHEOSTAT ILLUMINATION SWITCH/MULTI INFORMATION DISPLAY SWITCH/MULTI INFORMATION DISPLAY BACK SWITCH OPERATION BUZZER <COLOUR LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY TYPE>
When the rheostat illumination switch, multi information display switch and multi information display back switch is operated, the buzzer sounds.

MULTI INFORMATION DISPLAY INTERRUPT DISPLAY BUZZER <COLOUR LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY TYPE>
When the interrupt display is displayed on the multi information display because of alarms, the buzzer sounds.

ETACS-ECU FUNCTION CUSTOMISATION BUZZER
When entering into the customise mode of ETACS-ECU functions or when the customisation is executed, the buzzer sounds in each case.

TURN-SIGNAL LAMP BUZZER
The buzzer sounds in synchronisation with the turn signal lamp operation.

SECURITY ALARM
Previous alarm
    Sounds when the system proceeds to an alarm standby state.
Main alarm
    Sounds as prior notice for several seconds before the system proceeds to a alarm state.

REST REMINDER BUZZER
When the time to take a rest which was set on the multi-information display is reached, the buzzer sounds.

ASTC PRE HIGH TEMPERATURE WARNING BUZZER/ASTC HIGH TEMPERATURE WARNING BUZZER <VEHICLES WITH ASTC>
Previous alarm
    The combination meter will sound the buzzer when the ASTC-ECU calculates a brake pad temperature and determines that the temperature exceeds a certain value.
Main alarm
    When the ASTC-ECU estimates a brake pad temperature, and determines that it is hot, the combination meter will sound the buzzer.

ELECTRIC PARKING BRAKE EMERGENCY BRAKE BUZZER <A/T>
The combination meter will sound the buzzer under any of the following conditions:

  • The emergency brake is triggered during driving and the driver pulls and holds the electric parking brake switch.
  • The electric parking brake is locked and the vehicle speed exceeds 2 km/h.
