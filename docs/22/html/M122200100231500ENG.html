DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> EXTENSION HOUSING REMOVAL

Pull the extension housing toward the rear while pushing the change shifter toward the right.

<<B>> COUNTERSHAFT LOCK NUT REMOVAL

1.Shift the 1st-2nd speed synchroniser sleeve to the second speed side and the OD-R synchroniser sleeve to the reverse side for double meshing. This prevents the countershaft from turning.
2.Remove the staked nut from the countershaft.
3.Remove the countershaft lock nut.

<<C>> COUNTERSHAFT REAR BEARING REMOVAL

Using the special tool Bearing and gear puller (MD998348) to remove the countershaft rear bearing.

<<D>> COUNTERSHAFT OVERDRIVE GEAR REMOVAL

Using the special tool Steering wheel puller (MB990803) to remove the countershaft overdrive gear.

<<E>> MAINSHAFT LOCK NUT REMOVAL

1.Shift the 1st-2nd speed synchroniser sleeve to the second speed side and the OD-R synchroniser sleeve to the reverse side for double meshing. This prevents the mainshaft from turning.
2.Remove the staked nut from the mainshaft.
3.Using the special tool Lock nut wrench (41) (MD998809) to remove the mainshaft lock nut.

<<F>> BEARING SLEEVE REMOVAL

1.Set special tools as shown in illustration.

  • Bearing puller (MD998020)
  • Bearing puller adapter (MD998028)
2.Screw the centre bolt of special tool, and pull out the bearing sleeve, together with OD-R synchroniser hub.

<<G>> COUNTERSHAFT CENTRE BEARING REMOVAL

1.Using the special tool Counter gear bearing puller (MD998192) to remove the countershaft centre bearing.

<<H>> MAINSHAFT CENTRE BEARING REMOVAL

1.Set special tool as shown in illustration.

  • Bearing puller (MD998020)
  • Bearing puller adapter (MD998028)
2.Screw the centre bolt of special tool, and pull out the mainshaft centre bearing.

<<I>> MAINDRIVE GEAR BEARING REMOVAL

1.Set special too as shown in illustration.

  • Bearing puller (MD998020)
2.Screw the centre bolt of special tool, and pull out the maindrive gear bearing.

<<J>> COUNTERSHAFT REMOVAL

To facilitate the removal of countershaft, set the 1st-2nd synchroniser sleeve to the 1st gear side and the 3rd-4th synchroniser sleeve to the 3rd gear side.

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< MAINDRIVE GEAR BEARING INSTALLATION

Using the special tools to install the maindrive gear bearing.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-100 (MD998813)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (42) (MD998820)

>>B<< SNAP RING INSTALLATION

1.Install the thickest snap ring that can be fitted in the snap ring groove of maindrive gear.
2.Make sure that the maindrive gear bearing axial play meet the standard value.
Standard value: 0 - 0.06 mm

>>C<< MAINSHAFT CENTRE BEARING INSTALLATION
caution When striking in the mainshaft centre bearing, pay attention to prevent jamming of the synchroniser ring and the each gear.

Using the special tools to install the mainshaft centre bearing.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (40) (MD998819)

>>D<< BEARING SLEEVE INSTALLATION

Use special tools to install the bearing sleeve.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (38) (MD998818)

>>E<< COUNTERSHAFT CENTRE BEARING INSTALLATION

Using the special tools to install the countershaft centre bearing.

  • Installer adapter (30) (MD998816)
  • Bearing installer (MD998199)

>>F<< ADJUSTMENT OF SPACER INSTALLATION (MAINDRIVE GEAR BEARING)
Install the selected spacer into the ball bearing.
(Refer to adjustment of transmission-spacer selection for adjustment of maindrive gear bearing axial play ).

>>G<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

1.Using special tool Oil seal installer (MD998200) install the oil seal into the front bearing retainer.

2.Apply specified grease to the lip of the front bearing retainer oil seal.
Specified lubricant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. 0101011 or equivalent

>>H<< FRONT BEARING RETAINER INSTALLATION

1.Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply the specified sealant to the entire circumference of the mounting surface of the front bearing retainer.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>I<< THRUST WASHER INSTALLATION

Install the thrust washer its polished side faced to reverse idler gear.

>>J<< SYNCHRONISER KEY/OD-R SYNCHRONISER HUB/SYNCHRONISER SPRING/OD-R SYNCHRONISER SLEEVE INSTALLATION

1.Before reassembly, make sure that the hub and sleeve faces the correct direction.

2.Insert the three keys into the grooves of the synchroniser hub.
3.Install two synchroniser springs to the synchroniser. When the installing the springs, be sure to position each spring with respect to the keys as illustrated.

4.Use special tools to install the synchroniser hub.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-100 (MD998813)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (38) (MD998818)

>>K<< BEARING SLEEVE INSTALLATION

1.Use the special tools to install the bearing sleeve.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-100 (MD998813)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (34) (MD998817)

>>L<< MAINSHAFT LOCK NUT INSTALLATION

1.Shift the 1st-2nd speed synchroniser sleeve to the second speed side and OD-R synchroniser sleeve to the reverse side for double meshing. This presents the mainshaft from turning.
2.Using the special tool Lock nut wrench (41) (MD998809) tighten the mainshaft lock nut to specified torque of 255 ± 10 N·m.

3.Stake the two places of the lock nut as shown in illustration.
4.Ensure that the overdrive gear rotates smoothly.

>>M<< 1ST-2ND SPEED SHIFT RAIL/COUNTERSHAFT OVERDRIVE GEAR INSTALLATION

1.Install the 1st-2nd speed shift rail and countershaft overdrive gear simultaneously.

2.Use special tools to install the counter shaft overdrive gear.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-100 (MD998813)
  • Installer adapter (30) (MD998816)

>>N<< COUNTERSHAFT REAR BEARING INSTALLATION

Use special tools to install the countershaft rear bearing.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-100 (MD998813)
  • Installer adapter (30) (MD998816)

>>O<< COUNTERSHAFT LOCK NUT INSTALLATION

1.Tighten the countershaft lock nut to specified torque of 172 ± 14 N·m.

2.Stake the two places of the lock nut as shown in illustration.
3.Ensure that the OD gear rotates smoothly.

>>P<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION

Press in the spring pin so that its slit is parallel with the shift rail.

>>Q<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION

Press in the spring pin so that its slit is parallel with the shift rail.

>>R<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION

Press in the spring pin so that its slit is parallel with the shift rail.

>>S<< SPRING INSTALLATION

Insert the spring with small end on the steel ball side. Three springs are identical to each other.
Spring
 Identification colour
For 1ST-2ND speed shift rail
 Pink
For 3RD-4TH speed shift rail
 Yellow
For OD-R shift rail
 Pink

>>T<< PLUG INSTALLATION

1.Apply the specified sealant to the plug.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent
2.Screw plug as shown.

>>U<< SNAP RING/MAINSHAFT REAR BEARING INSTALLATION

1.Install a snap ring of the same size as having been used.

2.Use the special tools to install the mainshaft rear bearing.

  • Installer cap (MD998812)
  • Installer-200 (MD998814)
  • Installer adapter (34) (MD998817)

3.Install the thickest snap ring that can be fitted in the snap ring groove of mainshaft.
4.Make sure that the mainshaft rear bearing axial play meet the standard value.
Standard value: 0 - 0.13 mm

>>V<< UNDER COVER INSTALLATION

1.Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply the specified sealant to under cover.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>W<< EXTENSION HOUSING INSTALLATION

1.Install the extension housing while pushing the change shifter in the direction of the arrow and fit the control finger into the groove in the selector.

2.Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
3.Apply the specified sealant to extension housing as illustration.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>X<< SPECIAL BOLT/NUT/AIR BREATHER JOINT INSTALLATION

1.Apply the specified sealant to the bolt as shown.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

2.Apply the specified sealant to the nut threads.
Specified sealant:
3M AAD Part No. 2353 or equivalent

3.Apply the specified sealant to the air breather joint as shown.
Specified sealant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>Y<< SPEEDOMETER GEAR INSTALLATION

1.Apply the specified grease to the O-ring of the speedometer.
Specified lubricant:
MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. 01010111 or equivalent
2.Match the mating marks to the number to teeth on the speedometer driven gear. Install the speedometer gear.
