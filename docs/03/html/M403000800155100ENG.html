QUARTER OUTER


caution When repairing the area using sound dampening foam material do not use firing tools since the sound dampening foam material may burn.






NOTE ON REPAIR WORK

REMOVAL

When removing the quarter panel extension upper outer, to secure the strength of the rear pillar cut area, cut the quarter panel extension upper outer 50 mm below the side outer panel cut area.

INSTALLATION



1.When installing the new rear quarter panel outer, perform the following procedures.
(1) Machine the body loose panel cushion (6320A242 or 6320A254) for replacement as shown and apply it to the body side because the sound dampening foam material cannot be filled into the rear pillar.
(2) Make an appropriate pad from body loose panel cushion (6320A242 or 6320A254) as shown. To install a new quarter panel outer, apply this pad as a dam to prevent the sound dampening foam, which will be filled in the wheelhouse arch inner, from dripping down.
(3) Apply in advance body sealant to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

2.After the installation of quarter panel outer, seal up the hole and flange with the bolt and aluminium tape. Then, fill the sound dampening foam material from the hole shown in the figure.

    Sound dampening foam material:
    3M ATD Part No.08463

3.Wait 2 hours after filling the sound dampening foam material to remove the bolt and aluminium tape, then melt the sound dampening foam material with a soldering gun so a clip, etc. can thoroughly be inserted in the hole filled with sound dampening foam material.
