INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE HOSE ASSEMBLY CONNECTION INSTALLATION
1.Install so that the mating marks are facing rearward.

2.Place the pressure hose stopper to touch the power steering oil pump stopper, and install the pressure hose assembly.

>>B<< POWER STEERING OIL PUMP SUCTION HOSE CONNECTION INSTALLATION
1.Install the suction hose so that the marking is positioned upward.
