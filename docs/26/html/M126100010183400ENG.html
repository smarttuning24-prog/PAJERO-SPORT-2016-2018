GENERAL INFORMATION


OUTLINE OF CHANGE


Due to the change below, the service procedure has been added. The other service procedures are the same as before.

  • A front differential carrier without free wheel clutch has been introduced to 4WD vehicles <Vehicles without free wheel clutch>
  • Due to the introduction of the front differential carrier without free wheel clutch, the actuator, the solenoid valve, and the vacuum tank have been discontinued.
  • The vacuum pipe assembly, vacuum hose, and vacuum pipe have been changed.
  • The length and the maximum diameter of the inner shaft have been changed.
  • The 6-speed manual transmission is equipped with a freewheel clutch.
  • The vacuum tank is mounted by means of bolts instead of flange nuts.
