SENSORS


WHEEL SPEED SENSOR

The wheel speed sensor is composed of the rotor (for wheel speed detection) which rotates at the same speed of the wheels and the fixed wheel speed sensor, and it outputs pulse signals in proportion to wheel speed.

G & YAW RATE SENSOR
The G & yaw rate sensor is integrated in the ASTC-ECU, and detects the yaw rate and longitudinal and lateral acceleration of a vehicle.

STEERING WHEEL SENSOR
The steering wheel sensor is integrated in the column switch assembly. The steering wheel sensor is attached to the column switch, and detects the rotational angle of the steering wheel.

ASC OFF SWITCH

The ASC OFF switch is installed on the side air outlet (driver’s side). ASC, TSA and FCM functions can be disabled by pressing this switch for 3 seconds (The ATC, HSA, BA and Hill descent control function operates even when the ASC system is turned OFF with the ASC OFF switch). Pressing this switch again resumes the ON status. As a countermeasure for the stuck of the ASC OFF switch, pressing the ASC OFF switch for 15 seconds resumes the system to the ON status.
When the ignition switch is turned from "LOCK" (OFF) to ON position, ASC functions are constantly in the ON status.
