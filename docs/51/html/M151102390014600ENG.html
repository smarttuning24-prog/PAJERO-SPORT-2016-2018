DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> MIRROR REMOVAL

caution The tab of the mirror is prone to breakage when working in cold temperatures. Always use a hair drier or the like to warm up the mirror tab and its periphery to 20°C or higher prior to works. When the mirror is heated too quickly from its cold state, it may be broken.
1.Slant the mirror upward with your hands. Then insert a flat-tipped screwdriver wrapped with protective tape between the pivot plate and mirror. Now pry off the mirror tabs and release the lower side of the mirror as shown in the illustration.
2.Release the upper side of the mirror tabs while pulling out the mirror.
3.Disconnect the connectors of the heated mirror.

<<B>> SIDE VIEW CAMERA COVER REMOVAL

Remove the screw connecting the mirror housing and camera cover.

<<C>> TAPE REMOVAL

Remove the tape around the wiring harness shown in the illustration.

<<D>> CONNECTOR REMOVAL
Cut the wiring harness and disconnect the connector.
