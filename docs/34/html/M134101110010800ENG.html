LOWER ARM BUSHING REPLACEMENT



 1.Use the following special tools and the press to drive out the lower arm bushing.

  • Bushing remover and installer base (MB990891)
  • Lower arm bushing remover and installer base (MB990971)
  • Lower arm bushing arbour (MB991318)




2.Use the following special tools and the press to press fit the lower arm bushing.

  • Bushing remover and installer base (MB990891)
  • Lower arm bushing remover and installer base (MB990971)
  • Lower arm bushing arbour (MB991318)
  • Bushing remover and installer base (MB991180)
note
  • When press fitting the lower arm bushing, align the lower arm bushing projection with rear bushings as shown in the figure.
  • Equalize the protrusion amounts (dimension A) of lower arm bushings.
