TPMS WARNING LAMP CHECK



 1.Check that the TPMS warning lamp illuminates for approximately 3 seconds when the ignition switch is turned to the "ON" position.
2.Check that it illuminates for approximately 3 seconds and then goes out (If the warning lamp does not turn off and stays on, perform troubleshooting for TPMS Inspection Procedure No.1. Refer to .) (If the warning lamp does not turn off and flashes, perform troubleshooting for TPMS Inspection Procedure No.2. Refer to ).
3.If the TPMS fails or the tyre pressure is low, KOS&OSS-ECU warns the driver of that state by the TPMS warning lamp and the TPMS information display in the combination meter.
