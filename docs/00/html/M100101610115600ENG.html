  • The colours of the following connectors have been changed.

    • The J/C (CAN4) connector (D-101) has been changed from milk white to "B".

 INSTRUMENT PANEL
 RHD

  • The colours of the following connectors have been changed.

    • The J/C (CAN4) connector (D-101) has been changed from milk white to "B".
    • The J/C (CAN1) connector (D-111) has been changed from milk white to "B".
    • The J/C (CAN2) connector (D-141) has been changed from milk white to "B".
    • The J/C (8) connector (D-239) has been changed from milk white to "G".
    • The J/C (6) connector (D-244) has been changed from milk white to "B".

  • The remarks for the hazard warning lamp switch (D-125) has been changed from <Vehicles for General Export> to <Vehicles for General Export without passenger’s air bag cut off switch>.

  • The remarks for the hazard indicator assembly (D-129) has been changed from <Vehicles for Australia and New Zealand> to <Vehicles for Australia, New Zealand and vehicles with passenger’s air bag cut off switch>.

  • The passenger’s air bag cut off switch (D-150) has been added.

  • Due to the addition of the ion generator, the following connectors have been added.

    • A/C-ECU (D-157)
    • Ion generator (D-158)
    • Air refresher ionizer switch (D-159)
