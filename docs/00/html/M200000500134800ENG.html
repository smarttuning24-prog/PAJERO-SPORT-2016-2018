PRODUCT FEATURES


Delicate styling and elaborate design



  • The silhouette has an image of going through the earth more dynamically and aggressively.
  • The flexible and strong body-surface with a high-density image.
  • Spacious passenger compartment due to a delicate design
  • Comfortable space with high quality and sporty image.
  • The elegant shape of silver ornamentation with a feel of dynamic movement.
  • Improved switches and controls specially designed for new systems

    • OSS (One-push Start System) and KOS (Keyless Operation System)
    • Dial type 2WD/4WD switching
    • Electric Parking Brake
    • Multi around monitor

SUPERIOR SAFETY



  • RISE BODY (Realised Impact Safety Evolution) to which the new-generation ladder frame is adopted

  • Active stability and traction control system (ASTC)
  • Driver’s and passenger’s (front) air bags and seat belt with pre-tensioner
  • Side-airbag and the curtain air bag
  • To enhance safety, an ultrasonic misacceleration mitigation system and a blind spot warning system have been introduced.

HIGH OFF-ROAD ABILITY



  • Highly reliable 4WD system
  • Rear differential lock system
  • Wading depth up to 700 mm
