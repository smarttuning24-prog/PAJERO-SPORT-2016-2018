ACTUATORS


ELECTRIC PARKING BRAKE ACTUATOR



  • The electric parking brake actuator, which incorporates an electric motor and a tension sensor, regulates the parking brake by actuating the electric motor.
  • If the electric parking brake fails due to a flat battery or any other reason, it can be released by operating the electric parking brake actuator manually.

ELECTRIC PARKING BRAKE SWITCH INDICATOR LAMP AND COMBINATION METER



  • The electric parking brake ECU illuminates the indicator lamp on the electric parking brake switch as described in the table below.
  • The electric parking brake ECU illuminates the warning lamp on the combination meter as described in the table below. In addition, the ECU displays a warning notice on the multi-information display in the combination meter.
