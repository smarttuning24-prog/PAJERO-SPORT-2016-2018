REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> CATALYTIC CONVERTER REMOVAL
1.Remove some rubber hangers of the main muffler (the rearmost ones should be in situ) to allow sufficient clearance to remove the catalytic converter.
2.Rotate the catalytic converter 180° and take out it, being careful not to hit the crossmember.

<<B>> OXYGEN SENSOR REMOVAL
1.Remove the oxygen sensor connector connection, connector bracket and harness bracket.

2.Use special tool oxygen sensor wrench (MB991953) to remove the oxygen sensor.

<<C>> EXHAUST CENTRE PIPE REMOVAL
Remove some rubber hangers of the main muffler (the rearmost ones should be in situ) to allow sufficient clearance to remove the exhaust centre pipe.
