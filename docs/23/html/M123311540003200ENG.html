DISASSEMBLY


1. OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

1.Remove the 20 bolts.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14.5 mm

2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the fitting surface of the transmission case and the oil pan sub-assembly.
  • Be careful not to deform the oil pan sub-assembly.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the oil pan sub-assembly to remove it.
3.Remove the oil pan gasket.

2. OIL CLEANER MAGNET REMOVAL

Remove the 4 oil cleaner magnets from the oil pan sub-assembly.
note Examine the chips and particles in the oil pan sub-assembly and on the oil cleaner magnet to determine what type of wear has occurred in the transmission.
Steel (magnetic) = bearing, gear and plate wear.
Brass (non-magnetic) = bushing wear.

12. PARKING LOCK PAWL BRACKET REMOVAL

Remove the 3 bolts to remove the parking lock pawl bracket.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

13. PARKING LOCK ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

Disconnect the parking lock rod sub-assembly from the manual valve lever sub-assembly.

14. PARKING LOCK PAWL REMOVAL

1.
caution Be careful not to apply too much force to the torsion spring.
Pull out the parking lock pawl shaft and the torsion spring.
2.Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the parking lock pawl shaft.
3.Remove the parking lock pawl.

15. MANUAL VALVE LEVER SHAFT REMOVAL

1.
caution Be careful not to damage the manual valve lever sub-assembly.
Using a hammer and a screwdriver, cut off the spacer to remove it.

2.Using a hammer and a pin punch, drive out the spring pin.

3.Pull the manual valve lever shaft out through the transmission case, and remove the manual valve lever sub-assembly.

16. OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution Be careful not to damage the transmission case.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 oil seals.

REASSEMBLY

1. OIL SEAL INSTALLATION
caution Be careful not to damage the transmission case.

Using the special tool Drag link bushing remove and installer B (MB990842) and a hammer, install the 2 new oil seals.

2. MANUAL VALVE LEVER SHAFT INSTALLATION

1.Install the new spacer to the manual valve lever sub-assembly.
2.Install the manual valve lever shaft to the transmission assembly through the manual valve lever sub-assembly.

3.Using a hammer, drive in the new spring pin.

4.Align the manual valve lever shaft indentation with the spacer hole, and stake them together with the punch.
5.Make sure that the manual valve lever shaft rotates smoothly.

3. PARKING LOCK PAWL INSTALLATION

1.Install the parking lock pawl.
2.Install the snap ring to the parking lock pawl shaft.
3.
caution Be careful not to apply too much force to the torsion spring.
Install the parking lock pawl shaft and the torsion spring.

4. PARKING LOCK ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

Connect the parking lock rod sub-assembly to the manual valve lever sub-assembly.

5. PARKING LOCK PAWL BRACKET INSTALLATION

1.Install the parking lock pawl bracket with the 3 bolts.
2.Tighten the parking lock pawl bracket mounting bolts to the specified torque 7.4 ± 1.4 N·m.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

3.Shift the manual valve lever sub-assembly to the P position, and confirm the RR planetary gear assembly is correctly locked up by the parking lock pawl.

11. VALVE BODY ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Install the parking lock rod sub-assembly to the manual valve lever sub-assembly as shown in the illustration.
2.
caution
  • When installing, be sure to put the harness in the concave portion of the separator plate in the valve body assembly as shown in the illustration.
  • Do not pinch the harness between the separator plate and the valve body assembly.
Install the valve body assembly.
3.Temporarily install the valve body assembly with the 19 bolts.
SIZE: Bolt
A: M6 x 1.0 x 25 mm
B: M6 x 1.0 x 36 mm
note Aligning the bolt holes, temporarily tighten the 19 bolts by hand.

4.Tighten the valve body assembly mounting bolts to the specified torque 11 ± 1 N·m.
note Be sure to tighten the inner bolts first.

12. MANUAL DETENT SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Install the manual detent spring sub-assembly and the manual detent spring cover with the flange bolt to the valve body assembly.
2.Tighten the manual detent spring sub-assembly and the manual detent spring cover mounting flange bolt to the specified torque 10 ± 2 N·m.
SIZE: Flange bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14 mm

5.Tighten the temperature sensor clamp mounting bolt to the specified torque.

15. OIL CLEANER MAGNET INSTALLATION

Install the 4 oil cleaner magnets to the oil pan assembly.
note Clean the oil cleaner magnet before install it.

16. CLEAN CONTACT SURFACE
caution Make sure that no foreign matter is on the contact surfaces to ensure a secure fit between the oil pan assembly and transmission case.
Clean the contact surfaces of the oil pan assembly and transmission case.

17. OIL PAN ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the fitting surfaces of the transmission case and the oil pan assembly.
  • Be careful not to deform the oil pan assembly.
Install a new oil pan gasket and the oil pan assembly to the transmission case.
2.
caution Be reminded that bolts might be damaged if tightened too much since the oil pan gasket is cork-made and there is little tightening sense.
Install the 20 bolts to the transmission case.
3.Tighten the oil pan assembly mounting bolts to the specified torque 7.0 ± 0.9 N·m.
SIZE: Bolt
M6 x 1.0 x 14.5 mm
