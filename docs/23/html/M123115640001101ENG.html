Inspection procedure
 Check conditions
 Items to be checked (Normal conditions)
 Possible cause of abnormality
1
 Brake pedal not depressed
 Ignition switch position: Other than "ON"
 The selector lever cannot be moved out of the "P" position
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
  • Abnormality in the electrical circuit
2
 Brake pedal depressed
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
  • Abnormality in the electrical circuit
3
 Brake pedal not depressed
 Ignition switch position: "ON"
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
  • Electric circuit related to the shift lock solenoid fails
  • Electric circuit related to the stop lamp switch fails
  • The ETACS-ECU may be defective
4
 Brake pedal depressed
 The selector lever can be moved from the "P" position to other positions smoothly.
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
  • Electric circuit related to the shift lock solenoid fails
  • Electric circuit related to the stop lamp switch fails
  • The ETACS-ECU may be defective
5
 Shift lock release button pressed
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
6
 Brake pedal depressed / not depressed
 The selector lever can be moved from the "R" position to the "P" position smoothly.
  • Abnormality in the shift lock link (stuck, disengaged, broken, etc.)
7
 Selector lever: Other than P range
 The ignition switch cannot be turned to the LOCK (OFF) position.
  • Electric circuit related to the P position detection switch fails
8
 Selector lever: Do not touch the lever at P range.
 The ignition switch can be turned to the LOCK (OFF) position.
  • Electric circuit related to the P position detection switch fails

COMPONENT PARTS CHECK

SHIFT LOCK LINK
Check each part for damage and disengagement of the selector lever assembly.

SHIFT LOCK SOLENOID

1.Measure the resistance between terminal No.3 and No.4 of the selector lever assembly connector.
Standard value: 40 ± 2 Ω
2.When the resistance is outside the standard value, replace the selector lever assembly.

P POSITION DETECT SWITCH CHECK
Check the continuity at the selector lever assembly connector.
