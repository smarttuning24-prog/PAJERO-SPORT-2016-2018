Inspection Procedure 16: Odour, White Smoke, Black Smoke and High-Concentration CO/HC during Idling


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM



  • Failure is possibly caused by improper air-fuel ratio, deteriorated catalyst, and failed ignition system or fuel system, or improper compression pressure, and so on.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed air-fuel ratio control system
  • Failed ignition system
  • Failed fuel system
  • Failed intake and exhaust air system
  • Improper compression pressure
  • Deteriorated catalyst
  • Failed engine-ECU

STEP 7. Check throttle body (throttle valve portion) contamination.

Q. Is the check result normal?
YES : Go to Step 10.

STEP 10. Fuel pressure measurement.

  • Fuel pressure measurement. (Refer to ).

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 11.
Repair.

STEP 11. Check positive crankcase ventilation valve itself.

  • Check positive crankcase ventilation valve itself (Refer to GROUP 17 - Emission Control - Crankcase Emission Control System - Positive Crankcase Ventilation Valve Check).

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 12.
Replace the positive crankcase ventilation valve.

STEP 12. Visual check of ignition spark.

  • Remove the spark plug and install it to the ignition coil.
  • Connect the ignition coil connector.
  • Remove all injector connectors.
  • At the engine start, check each spark plug produces a spark.

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 13.
Check Inspection Procedure - ignition circuit system (Refer to ).

STEP 13. Check compression pressure.

  • Check the compression pressure (Refer to GROUP 11C - On-vehicle Service - Compression Pressure Check ).

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 14.
Repair.

STEP 14. Check injector for spray condition.

  • Check each injector for spray condition (Refer to ).

Q. Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 15.
Replace the injector.

STEP 15. Replace the catalytic converter.

  • After replacing catalytic converter check the trouble symptom again.

Q. Does the trouble symptom persist?
Replace the engine-ECU.
Check end.
