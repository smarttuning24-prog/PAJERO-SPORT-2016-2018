TEST MODE


The e-Call switch allows the indicator, the speaker or the microphone to enter a respective test mode.
note When you test a component by means of a test mode, you should park the vehicle in a well-ventilated area.
1.When the ignition switch or the operation mode is set to OFF, set the ignition switch or operation mode ON while pressing the e-Call switch.
2.Perform Step 3 and 4 within 20 seconds after the red lamp and green lamp illuminate.
3.Release finger from the e-Call switch.
4.Press the e-Call switch 3 or more times.
5.After approximately 20 seconds from Step 2, the red lamp and green lamp extinguish and buzzer sounds 3 times.
note If the buzzer does not sound, repeat the process from Step 1.
6.Approximately 60 seconds later, the green lamp flash. If press the e-Call switch within 20 seconds, the mode changes to the test mode.
7.Within approximately 20 seconds after the mode is changed to the test mode, press the e-Call switch for one of the following periods to select the desired test type.

REPORT TEST TO THE EMERGENCY CALL CENTRE or TEST OF THE ERA-GLONASS SYSTEM EQUIPMENT OF THE VEHICLE




Test type
 Test contents
 Test results
Indicator test
 The buzzer sounds once, and then the red lamp and green lamp flash alternately every one second.
 OK (All tests): The buzzer sounds once, and the green lamp illuminates for 5 seconds.
NG (Either of the tests): The buzzer sounds three times, and the red lamp illuminates for 5 seconds.
Speaker check
 The buzzer sounds twice, and the passenger’s side door speaker* beeps.
Microphone check
 The buzzer sounds three times, and your voice is played though the microphone to the passenger’s side door speaker*.
Test report
 Checks the telephone line.
note *: When diagnosis code B1856 and B1864 are set, the e-Call speaker will respond instead.
