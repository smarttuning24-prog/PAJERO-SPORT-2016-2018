Code No. C2226 BSW switch


OPERATION


The clearance warning control unit sets diagnosis code No. C2226 when it receives blind spot warning switch ON-signal for at least one minute, or when it detects any open circuit or short to earth in the blind spot warning indicator circuit while the sonar switch indicator is deactivated.

TROUBLE JUDGEMENT


Any of the conditions below is satisfied:

  • The blind spot warning switch signal remains ON for at least one minute.
  • The system detects a malfunction for at least three seconds when the blind spot warning switch indicator is deactivated.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of blind spot warning switch
  • Malfunction of clearance warning control unit
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
