REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION


caution
  • When you replace the camera-ECU, you should teach the camera-ECU for the multi-around monitor by writing a learned value. On completion, you should check the camera image on the multi around monitor "Bird’s eye-view." If necessary, calibrate the camera (Refer to ).
  • To ensure the camera image is clearly visible, wipe the lens with a damped rag and then a dry rag to remove dust, rain drops or snow.
  • Be careful not to damage the camera. Any damage may have an adverse effect on the screen image.
