MITSUBISHI MULTI ENTERTAINMENT SYSTEM (MMES)


The multivision display with integrated flash memory drive (SD) has been fitted (SD Navigation).

Main unit specification





  • 7-inch touch panel monitor
    (panel tilted by six steps; 5, 10, 15, 20, 25 and 30 degrees) to decrease the reflection of sunlight.
  • With integrated SD(8 GB) (for map data)
  • With integrated SD card slots (audio and map)
  • With integrated DVD/CD drive
  • With integrated AM/FM radio tuner
  • With integrated 47W x 4-channel amplifier
  • With integrated Bluetooth® module, antenna and microphone (for hands-free cellular phone system)
  • Supports iPod®/portable music player
  • Rear view image is displayed <Vehicles without multi around monitor>
  • Multi around monitor image is displayed <Vehicles with multi around monitor>
