GENERAL INFORMATION


OUTLINE OF CHANGES


Due to the change below, the service procedure has been added. The other service procedures are the same as before.

  • A rear disc brake has been introduced as an option. Due to this, a rear axle shaft for rear disc brake has been introduced. <4D5>
  • A hybrid type LSD (Limited Slip Differential) rear differential has been introduced as an option. <Vehicles for General Export, South Africa and GCC>
