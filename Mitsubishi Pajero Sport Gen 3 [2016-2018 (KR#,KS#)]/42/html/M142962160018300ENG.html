Code No.B1136 Stop lamp switch


caution When replacing the ECU, always check that the communication circuit is normal.

DIAGNOSTIC FUNCTION


When the stop lamp switch status is different from the brake switch information received from ETACS-ECU via CAN, KOS&OSS-ECU sets diagnosis code No.B1136, and displays the power supply system error warning the combination meter, and flashes the indicator of engine switch in orange.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA


Check Conditions
  • After KOS&OSS-ECU activation
Judgement Criterion
  • The difference between the stop lamp switch condition and the stop lamp switch information has been detected.
  • The stop lamp switch information from the ETACS-ECU cannot be received via CAN communication.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • CAN bus line malfunction
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector (short to earth or open circuit between stop lamp switch and KOS&OSS-ECU or between stop lamp switch and ETACS-ECU)
  • ETACS-ECU malfunction
  • Malfunction of KOS&OSS-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics


After performing the forced power supply output (Refer to ) "IG-ON" using the M.U.T.-III, diagnose the CAN bus line.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.

Repair the CAN bus line (Refer to GROUP 54C - Troubleshooting ).

STEP 2. Check of short to earth or open circuit in STP1 line between stop lamp switch connector and KOS&OSS-ECU connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.

Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.

STEP 3. Check of short to earth or open circuit in BKLP line between stop lamp switch connector and ETACS-ECU connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.

Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.

STEP 4. M.U.T.-III other system data list


Check the ETACS-ECU data list.

  • Item No.108: Stop lamp switch (Refer to GROUP 54A - ETACS, Service Data Reference Table ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.

Diagnose the ETACS-ECU (Refer to GROUP 54A - ETACS, Trouble Symptom Chart for Input Signal ).

STEP 5. M.U.T.-III data list
