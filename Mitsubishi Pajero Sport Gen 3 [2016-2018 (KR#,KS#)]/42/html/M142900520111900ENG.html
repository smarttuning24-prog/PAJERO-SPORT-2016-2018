INSPECTION PROCEDURE 6: Power window anti-trap function does not work normally <Driver’s side door>.


caution Before replacing the ECU, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


Malfunction of the power window motor revolution detection sensor or incorrectly installed or warped glass slider is suspected.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the power window motor
  • Incorrectly installed or warped glass slider
  • Improperly adjusted door window glass

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the power window fully closed position


(1)Carry out the learning procedures of the power window fully closed position (Refer to ).
(2)Fully open the door window glass. Operate the power window switch to fully close the door window glass, and check if the door window glass raise without lowering automatically.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.

Refer to adjustment of faulty power window (Refer to ).

STEP 2. Check of short to power supply, short to earth, and open circuit in VCC, SEN1, SEN2, SGND line between the power window main switch connector and the front power window motor (RH) connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.

Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 3. Retest the system.


Check that the power window anti-trap function works.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).

Replace the power window motor of the door which the power window anti-trap function does not work on.
