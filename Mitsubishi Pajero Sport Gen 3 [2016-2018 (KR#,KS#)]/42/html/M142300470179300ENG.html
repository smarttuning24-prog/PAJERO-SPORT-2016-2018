DOOR LATCH ASSEMBLY INSPECTION


Check the door lock actuator operation in the following procedures:
1.Open a door that is supposed to be checked.
2.Remove a door trim assembly (Refer to GROUP 52A - Door Trim ).
3.Use a screwdriver or the like to slide the door latch claw from unlatch to full-latch position (Set the door latch from door-open to door-closed condition).
