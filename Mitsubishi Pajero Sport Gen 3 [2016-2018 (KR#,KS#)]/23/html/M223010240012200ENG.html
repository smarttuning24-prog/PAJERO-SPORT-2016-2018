GENERAL INFORMATION


The R8AWG/V8AWG automatic transmission consists of the torque converter, gear train, oil pressure control mechanism, and electric control system.
The 3-element, 1-stage, 2-phase type torque converter with a built-in lock-up clutch is adopted.
The gear train consists of 4 sets of multi-disk type clutches, 2 sets of multi-disk type brakes, 1 one-way clutch, and 3 sets of planetary gears which consists of the sun gear, pinion gear, and ring gear.
