DISASSEMBLY

1. OIL PUMP ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

1.Remove the 10 bolts from the transmission case.
SIZE: Bolt
M8 x 1.25 x 40 mm

2.
caution Be careful not to drop the oil pump assembly from the transmission case.
Using the special tool Oil pump puller (MD998212), remove the oil pump assembly.

3.Remove the O-ring from the oil pump assembly.

SIZE: O-ring
Inner diameter (mm)
 Thickness (mm)
218.40
 3.50
4.Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 1 from the oil pump assembly.

2. FORWARD CLUTCH ASSEMBLY & DIRECT CLUTCH ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

1.
caution
  • Be careful not to drop the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.
  • Be careful not to drop the one-way clutch No. 3.
Remove the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly with the one-way clutch No. 3.

2.Remove the thrust bearing race and the thrust needle roller bearing from the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.

3.Remove the clutch hub thrust washer, one-way clutch No. 2 and the clutch drum thrust washer No. 2 from the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.

3. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 3 (B3) REMOVAL

1.Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the transmission case.

2.Remove the flange, 4 discs, 4 plates and cushion plate.
note Check the number of disc. (Refer to OIL PUMP AND CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY INSPECTION-INSPECT BRAKE DISK SET No. 3 (B3) )

4. BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 3 & SECOND BRAKE PISTON & SECOND BRAKE CYLINDER REMOVAL

1.Remove the hole snap ring from the transmission case.

2.Remove the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder with the one-way clutch No. 1, planetary carrier thrust washer No. 1 and the one-way clutch inner sub-assembly.

3.Remove the one-way clutch inner race sub-assembly and the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 1
4.Remove the one-way clutch No. 1 from the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder.

5. FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY REMOVAL

Remove the FR planetary gear assembly and the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 2.

6. FR PLANETARY RING GEAR & FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE & CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR REMOVAL

1.Remove the FR planetary ring gear & FR planetary gear flange & centre planetary ring gear.
2.Remove the thrust bearing race No. 3 and the thrust needle roller bearing from the FR planetary ring gear & FR planetary ring gear flange & centre planetary ring gear.

7. CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY AND PLANETARY SUN GEAR REMOVAL

1.Remove the centre planetary gear assembly and planetary sun gear.
2.Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 4 from the centre planetary gear assembly.

REASSEMBLY


RACE AND BEARING SPECIFICATIONS

1. CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY & PLANETARY SUN GEAR INSTALLATION

1.Install the thrust bearing race No. 4 and the thrust needle roller bearing to the centre planetary gear assembly.
2.Install the centre planetary gear assembly & planetary sun gear.

2. FR PLANETARY RING GEAR & FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE & CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR INSTALLATION

1.Apply ATF to the thrust bearing race No. 3 and the thrust needle roller bearing and install them to the FR planetary gear.
2.Install the FR planetary ring gear & FR planetary ring gear flange & centre planetary ring gear.

3. FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Apply ATF to the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 2 and install it to the FR planetary gear assembly.
2.Install the FR planetary gear assembly.

4. ONE-WAY CLUTCH INNER RACE SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Apply ATF to the planetary carrier thrust washer No. 1 and install it to the one-way clutch inner race sub-assembly.
2.Install the one-way clutch No. 1 to the one-way clutch inner race sub-assembly.
3.Install the one-way clutch inner race sub-assembly.

5. SECOND BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 3 & SECOND BRAKE PISTON & SECOND BRAKE CYLINDER INSTALLATION

1.
caution Align the hole of the second brake piston return spring sub-assembly, No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder with the position shown in the illustration.
Install the second brake piston return spring sub-assembly, No. 3 & second brake piston & second brake cylinder to the transmission case as shown in the illustration.

2.
caution If the hole snap ring is not installed in the correct direction, the automatic transmission may not work properly.
Check that the hole snap ring is installed in the correct direction.


3.
caution Align the opening of the hole snap ring with the position shown in the illustration.
Install the hole snap ring in the groove.

6. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 3 (B3) INSTALLATION


1.
caution Check the number and order of the cushion plate, the plates, the discs and the flange.
Install the cushion plate and plates, the discs and the flange to the transmission case as shown in the illustration.
note Install the cushion plate and plates, discs and flange following the order shown in the illustration.


2.
caution Align the opening of the hole snap ring with the position shown in the illustration.
Install the hole snap ring in the groove.

7. FORWARD CLUTCH ASSEMBLY & DIRECT CLUTCH ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Apply ATF to the clutch drum thrust washer No. 2 & clutch hub thrust washer, and install it to the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.
2.Install the one-way clutch No. 2.
3.Apply ATF to the clutch drum thrust washer No. 2 & clutch hub thrust washer and install it to the one-way clutch No. 2.

4.Apply ATF to the thrust bearing race and the thrust needle roller bearing and install them to the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.

5.
caution
  • Be careful not to drop the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.
  • Be careful not to drop the one-way clutch No. 2.
Install the forward clutch assembly & direct clutch assembly.

8. OIL PUMP ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

1.Coat the new O-ring with ATF, and install it to the oil pump assembly.
Inner diameter (mm)
 Thickness (mm)
218.40
 3.50
2.Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust bearing race No. 1 and install them to the oil pump assembly.

3.
caution Be careful not to drop the oil pump assembly.
Install the oil pump assembly.

4.Apply sealant to the 10 bolts and install them to the oil pump assembly.
Specified sealant:
Mitsubishi Part No. MS990188 or equivalent
5.Tighten the oil pump assembly mounting bolts to the specified torque 21 ± 3 N·m.
SIZE: Bolt
M8 x 1.25 x 40 mm
