REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

<<A>> SUPPORT THE MANUAL TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY

1.<5M/T>
    Use a transmission jack to support the manual transmission assembly.

2.<6M/T>
  1. Place a 10-mm thick wooden piece on the transmission jack attachment (MB991927) before attaching it to the transfer.
  2. Place the transmission assembly on the transmission jack attachment (MB991927).
  3. Support the transmission assembly.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT

>>A<< TRANSFER ROLL STOPPER INSTALLATION
1.Tighten the transfer roll stopper provisionally.

2.Tighten the transfer roll stopper inner and outer so that the clearance (A) between them is as shown in the illustration.
3.Check that the clearance (B) between the stoppers are the same.
note When new, a transfer roll stopper consists of one part, but will separate into outer and inner part during use.This is normal.
