INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT

>>A<< TRANSMISSION CONTROL LEVER ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION <R5MB1>

1.Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply a 1.2 mm diameter bead of liquid gasket to the intermediate plate mounting surface of the extension housing sub-assembly.
Special sealant: ThreeBond 1281B or equivalent

>>B<< SLEEVE INSTALLATION

Install the sleeve with its groove facing to the direction shown in the figure, and then install the shift knob.
