DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> H-L SHIFT RAIL REMOVAL

1.Fix the H-L shift rail at the high position.
2.Shift the 2-4WD shift rail at the 4WD position.
note If the 2-4WD shift rail is at the 2WD position, the chain cover cannot be removed because interlock is actuated.

<<B>> 2-4WD SYNCHRONISER / CHAIN / FRONT OUTPUT SHAFT REMOVAL

Remove the 2-4WD synchroniser, chain and front output shaft as a set.

ADJUSTMENT BEFORE REASSEMBLY


Spacer selection for adjustment of counter shaft axial play. (Refer to ADJUSTMENT OF TRANSFER - SPACER SELECTION FOR ADJUSTMENT OF COUNTER SHAFT AXIAL PLAY .)

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

1.Use special tools to install the oil seal.

  • Installer adapter (MB990931)
  • Installer bar (MB990938)
2.Apply the specified grease to the lip of oil seal.
Specified lubricant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. 0101011 or equivalent

>>B<< H-L CLUTCH HUB INSTALLATION

Check that the H-L clutch hub is in the correct direction for installation.

>>C<< SNAP RING INSTALLATION

1.Install the thickest snap ring that can be fitted in the snap ring groove of the rear output shaft.
2.Make sure that the H-L clutch hub axial play meets the standard value.
Standard value: 0 - 0.08 mm

>>D<< SNAP RING INSTALLATION

1.Install the thickest snap ring that can be fitted in the snap ring groove of the transfer drive shaft.
2.Make sure that the differential lock hub axial play meets the standard value.
Standard value: 0 - 0.08 mm

>>E<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION

1.Align the 2-4WD shift lug with the 2-4WD shift rail spring pin hole.
2.Hammer in the spring valve so that the spring pin slit matches the centre of the shift rail shaft.

>>F<< 2-4WD SYNCHRONISER / CHAIN / FRONT OUTPUT SHAFT INSTALLATION

1.Make a white paint mark on the deep groove portions (three places) of the 2-4WD synchroniser.
2.Make a white paint mark on the portions (three places) of the 2-4WD synchroniser sleeve splines.

3.Place the chain in tight mesh with the 2-4WD synchroniser and front output shaft sprockets.
4.With both sprockets spaced the farthest apart, install them on the transfer case simultaneously.

>>G<< CHAIN COVER INSTALLATION

1.
caution
    Squeeze the sealant out evenly to apply it in an unbroken, non-excessive quantity.
Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply sealant to the chain cover, and then install the chain cover.
Specified sealant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>H<< H-L CLUTCH SLEEVE INSTALLATION

Check that the H-L clutch sleeve is in the correct direction for installation.

>>I<< H-L SHIFT RAIL INSTALLATION

1.Tilt the transfer case with the 2-4WD shift rail downward as shown in the illustration.
2.Move the 2-4WD shift rail in the direction of the arrow fully.
3.After making sure that the plunger is not exposed out of the H-L shift rail hole in the transfer case, install the H-L shift rail.

>>J<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION

1.Align the spring pin holes on the shift rail with the shift fork. Then tap in the spring pin so that the slit of the spring pin is facing the shaft centre of the shift rail.

>>K<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

1.Use special tools to install the oil seal.

  • Installer adapter (MB990929)
  • Installer bar (MB990938)
2.Apply the specified grease to the lip of oil seal.
Specified lubricant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. 0101011 or equivalent

>>L<< SPACER INSTALLATION

1.Measure projection A of the rear output shaft bearing and depth B of the second stage of the rear cover.
2.Subtract A from B and let the answer be C. Select a spacer so that the subtracted value will be the standard value shown below.
Standard value: 0.025 - 0.150 mm

>>M<< REAR COVER INSTALLATION

1.
caution
    Squeeze the sealant out evenly to apply it in an unbroken, non-excessive quantity.
Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply the sealant to the chain cover.
Specified sealant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent

>>N<< SPACER INSTALLATION
Install the previously selected spacer (Refer to ADJUSTMENT BEFORE ASSEMBLY).

>>O<< COUNTER SHAFT GEAR INSTALLATION

1.Turn the input gear’s notched selection surface toward the counter shaft mounting surface. then install the counter shaft.

>>P<< TRANSFER CASE PLATE INSTALLATION

1.
caution
    Squeeze the sealant out evenly to apply it in an unbroken, non-excessive quantity.
Completely degrease the FIPG-applied surface so that water and oil including the old sealant cannot adhere to the surface coated with the sealant.
Never touch the degreased surface by hand.
2.Apply the sealant to the transfer case plate.
Specified sealant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MD997740 or equivalent
3.
caution
    The transfer case plate must be installed smooth and straight to get a good seal.
Install the transfer case plate together with the input gear and countershaft.

>>Q<< SPEEDOMETER DRIVEN GEAR SLEEVE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION

Match the mating marks to the number of teeth.

>>R<< SPRING INSTALLATION

Install the spring with its tapered end toward the ball.

>>S<< PLUG INSTALLATION

Apply the sealant to the threads.
Specified sealant: 3M AAD part No.8672 or equivalent
note
    The new plug is precoated with sealant, so sealant does not need to be applied.
