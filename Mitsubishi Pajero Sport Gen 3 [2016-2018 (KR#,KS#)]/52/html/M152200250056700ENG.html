DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

<<A>> REMOVAL OF SECOND SEAT LEVER CONTROL CABLE

Put a mark in the position shown in view A to record the protrusion of the screw.

<<B>> RECLINING CABLE INNER/RECLINING CABLE OUTER/RECLINING LINK CABLE ASSEMBLY/SECOND SEAT LEVER CONTROL CABLE REMOVAL

Put a mark in the position shown in view A or view B to record the protrusion of the screw.

<<C>> HEADRESTRAINT GUIDE REMOVAL

Remove the headrest guides while pushing the on both sides inward.

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< SECOND SEAT LEVER CONTROL CABLE INSTALLATION

1.When installing the second seat lever control cable, protrude the screws to the position marked during the removal and fix them. Then, tumble the seatback and check that the control cable is fully released.

>>B<< RECLINING CABLE INNER/RECLINING CABLE OUTER/RECLINING LINK CABLE ASSEMBLY/SECOND SEAT LEVER CONTROL CABLE INSTALLATION

1.When installing the reclining cable inner, reclining cable outer, reclining link cable assembly, second seat lever control cable, protrude the screws to the position marked during the removal and fix them. Then, tumble the seatback and check that the control cable is fully released.
