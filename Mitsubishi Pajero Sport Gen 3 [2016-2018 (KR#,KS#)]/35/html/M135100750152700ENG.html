REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION


Pre-removal Operation
  • Loosening the parking brake cable adjusting nut (Refer to GROUP 36 - On-vehicle Service, Parking Brake Lever Stroke Check and Adjustment ).
  • Brake fluid draining

 Post-installation Operation
  • Brake fluid filling and air bleeding (Refer to ).
  • Parking brake lever stroke adjustment (Refer to GROUP 36 - On-vehicle Service, Parking Brake Lever Stroke Check and Adjustment ).

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

<<A>> RETAINER REMOVAL

Use a flat-tipped screwdriver or a similar tool to open up the retainer joint. Then remove the retainer.
