BRAKE PEDAL CHECK AND ADJUSTMENT


CLEARANCE BETWEEN BRAKE PEDAL AND RETAINER
1.Turn up the carpet, etc. under the brake pedal.

2.Pierce a needle or similar material to the silencer and melting sheets, and measure the thickness of "D" shown in the figure.
3.Start the engine, depress the brake pedal with approximately 500 N of force, and measure the distance "E" between the silencer and brake pedal shown in the figure.
4.Check that the total value of "D" and "E" measured in the previous items 2 and 3 is within the standard value.
Standard value (D + E):
70 mm or more <4D5>
(From the surface of dash panel to the face of pedal pad)
5.If the clearance is outside the standard value, check for air trapped in the brake line, thickness of the disc brake pad, clearance between the lining and the drum and dragging in the parking brake. And then adjust and replace defective parts as required.
6.Return the carpet etc. to its original position.
