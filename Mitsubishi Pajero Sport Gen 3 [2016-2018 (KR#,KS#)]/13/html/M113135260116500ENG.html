Inspection Procedure 22: Blower Switch System


OPERATION

  • Battery voltage is applied from AC/S terminal on the engine-ECU connector through the dual pressure switch to the A/C switch terminal on the heater control panel connector.
  • It is earthed from the blower switch terminal on the heater control panel connector through the blower switch to vehicle body.

FUNCTION

  • When the blower switch is not turned off with the dual pressure switch ON conditions satisfied, the blower switch ON signal is sent to the engine-ECU. The engine-ECU uses this signal to control the A/C compressor relay.

PROBABLE CAUSES

  • Failed blower switch of heater control unit
  • Failed dual pressure switch
  • Open/short circuit or harness damage in blower switch circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-ECU
