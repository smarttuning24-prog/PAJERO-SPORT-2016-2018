Code No. P0538: Fin Thermo Sensor Circuit High Input


OPERATION



  • A power voltage of 5 V is applied to the fin thermo sensor connector terminal from the engine-ECU connector terminal AC/T.
  • The power supply voltage is earthed from the air temperature sensor through the temperature controller to the engine-ECU connector terminal ACTE.

FUNCTION



  • The fin thermo sensor converts the evaporator surface temperature into a voltage signal, and sends the voltage to the engine-ECU.
  • In response to the signal, the engine-ECU controls the A/C compressor relay.
  • The fin thermo sensor is a kind of resistor, which has characteristics to reduce its resistance as air temperature rises. Therefore, the sensor output voltage varies with the air temperature, and becomes lower as the air temperature rises.

TROUBLE JUDGEMENT


Check Condition
  • After 2 seconds since the ignition switch is turned "ON" or since the completion of engine start.
Judgement Criterion
  • Fin thermo sensor output voltage is more than 4.9 V for 4 seconds.

FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION



  • None

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed fin thermo sensor
  • Open circuit or harness damage in fin thermo sensor circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-ECU
