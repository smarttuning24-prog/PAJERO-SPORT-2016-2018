REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

<<A>> EGR VALVE PIPE B/EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
When the EGR pipe B and EGR valve assembly are removed, must prevent the condensate water, oil, coolant water is not applied to the EGR motor by using waste cloth.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS

>>A<< EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY/EGR VALVE PIPE GASKET/EGR VALVE PIPE A INSTALLATION

1.Install each gasket with its projection being positioned as shown in the figure.
2.Temporarily tighten the EGR valve assembly to 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m.
3.Temporarily tighten EGR valve pipe A to 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m.

4.Tighten each mounting bolt to the specified torque in the order as shown in the figure.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
