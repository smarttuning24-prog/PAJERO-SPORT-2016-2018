SPECIAL FUNCTION TABLE


Event Record



  • Item No. 1 to 10: This function records up to 10 vehicle conditions upon the cancellation of the ACC system.
  • Item No. 11: This function records the operation status of the emergency braking control in FCM system. Note that weak braking assist operation may not be always recorded.
